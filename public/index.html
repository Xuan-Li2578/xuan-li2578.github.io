Xuan Li

Research Assistant

City University of Hong Kong

Hello, welcome to my homepage! I am currently a Research Assistant at the Department of Chemistry, City University of Hong Kong. My research focuses on 2D materials grows, characterization and establishing auto transfer platform.

I received my Bachelor’s degree in Polymer Materials and Engineering from Beijing University of Chemical Technology (2015–2019), and later completed my Master’s degree in Materials Engineering at Sun Yat-sen University (2019–2022) under the supervision of Prof. Lei Shi. During my master’s studies, I specialized in 2D materials and carbon nanotubes research.

Outside of academics, I love traveling and botany.
